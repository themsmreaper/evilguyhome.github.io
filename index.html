EVIL GUYS

What is Evil Guys?

Evil Guys is a card game where you are an evil villain, commanding an army of henchmen to defeat the opposing bosses who stand in your way. You utilize engines, gadgets, lands, contracts and deals to help your henchmen drain the HP of your opponents.

Engine

This is the engine. It is used to generate energy which is the what's used to pay for everything in Evil Guys.

Playable

The playable is who you play as (if the name didn't make it evident). You play as some sort of villain boss, each playable having different abilities, health and other trade-offs.

Henchman/Henchmen

The henchmen are your basic pawn card, similar to other TCGs. You use them to attack and they each have unique (or not so unique) abilities.

Contract

Contracts are your basic effect/spell card. It does something to someone or something. The effect can be healing, dealing damage, destroying or something else more unique. These are the only cards that can be played during interjection (other than some special cards).

Gadget

Gadgets are cards that have unique effects and must be piloted by a HM to activate said effect.

Land

Lands have an effect that effects the everybody/the entire gameplay. Each player can only have one.
