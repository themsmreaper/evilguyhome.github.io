EVIL GUYS

What is Evil Guys?

Evil Guys is a card game where you are an evil villain, commanding an army of henchmen to defeat the opposing bosses who stand in your way. You utilize engines, gadgets, lands, contracts and deals to help your henchmen drain the HP of your opponents.

Playable

The playable is who you play as (if the name didn't make it evident). You play as some sort of villain boss, each playable having different abilities, health and other trade-offs.

Henchman/Henchmen

The henchmen are your basic pawn card, similar to other TCGs. You use them to attack and they each have unique (or not so unique) abilities. (skinny)
